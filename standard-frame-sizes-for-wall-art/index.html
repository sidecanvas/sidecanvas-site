Standard Frame Sizes for Wall Art

If you’re looking to add some personality to your home, hanging wall art can be an easy and affordable option.

While planning the arrangement of your hanging wall art pieces, it’s wise to be aware of standard frame sizes. Planning around standard dimensions keep costs low and save you the hassle of ordering custom frames. Even if you aren’t intending to frame your art, many prints and posters are sized to fit standard frames.

Most common mat board sizes

The most common frames will also be compatible with standard mat board. Like frames, mats also have standard sizes.

Mat openings sizes are cut so they are compatible with common print sizes. Standard mat openings are usually cut about 1/2 inch smaller than the print to “crop” the edge of the image for a cleaner look.
