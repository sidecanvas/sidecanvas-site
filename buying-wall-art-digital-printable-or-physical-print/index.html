Buying Wall Art: Digital Printable or Physical Print?

When purchasing décor for your walls, you can choose between buying digital printable or physical prints. Not sure which option to choose? This guide provides a quick comparison between the benefits of buying printable versus print.

Reasons to buy printable wall art

Cheaper

Buying art and printing it yourself is usually more affordable because you aren’t paying for the effort to print and ship. You can potentially print multiple art pieces yourself for the price of one physical print. This means more artwork for you to rotate between throughout the seasons!

Flexible adjustments

Having a digital copy of the artwork means more flexibility to make minor tweaks and adjustments. You are in control of printing, so you can choose the print medium, quality, dimension, and size. If you’re familiar with photo editing, you can also adjust colors.

While printable art can be printed right away without any adjustments, it’s still nice to have the option to make some tweaks here and there. For example, if you’re a perfectionist and you want to arrange your wall décor just the way you envision it, you might need to make a few adjustments to get it just right.

Fast or instant delivery

Shopping for digital art can be satisfying. With just a click of a button, you can download and access your purchase instantly. From there, you can choose to print at home, online, or a local print shop. If you want your print as soon as possible, you can print at home or visit your local print shop. If you don’t have access to a printer, then you can order a custom print online. You are in control of how and when your new wall décor reaches your fingertips.

Easy access to backup or extra prints

With digital art, you can create multiple prints. If you somehow lose or damage your wall art, you can always print another. Remember to save your digital file somewhere safe in case you need to reprint. As a bonus, you can also create a few extra prints to gift to your friends and family.

Reasons to buy wall art print

Convenient

Purchasing printed wall art is super simple. Simply order online and wait for the package to show up at your door. All of the printing is done for you. All you have to do is figure out where to hang your new wall décor.

Unboxing experience

If you’ve ever bought anything online, you’re probably familiar with the excitement and satisfaction of receiving your item. First, there’s the anticipation of waiting for your purchase to arrive at your doorstep. Then there’s the thrill of unboxing and feeling the object in your hand. This unboxing experience applies to art prints as well.

There is nothing like taking your new print out of its packaging and admiring it for the first time. After all, seeing the artwork in person is very different from seeing it through a screen.

No technical skills required

When buying wall art prints, technical skills beyond making online purchases are not needed. If you aren’t comfortable with printing art yourself, buying prints is the way to go.

High print quality

Since the artist is responsible for the printing, you can be sure that the art will be printed in the way it was intended to be printed. This means great print quality and the best-suited print mediums.
